About Us

Mekar Lalunna

Mekar Lalunna is a company that sells unique candles to our customers. The company was founded in 2020 and currently employs eight employees. Our company is called Mekar Lalunna, which means "blooming branch of heaven." The Malay Dictionary defines "mekar" as "to bloom, begin to grow, and decompose." Lalunna is a Hawaiian word that means "a branch of heaven."

The name of our company is strongly tied to what we market, which is candles with flowers in it. Fresh flowers that have been dried and placed into the candle are used in our candles. We want people to enjoy the smell provided by the candle they have purchased. Customers can get a candle with a long-lasting aroma for a reasonable price to use on a daily or special event. Aromatherapy can be used to ease headaches and tension using this smell. These candles can be used in any space, such as the living room, bedroom, bathroom, kitchen, or office.

We use high-grade ingredients to manufacture great candles. We are optimistic that the fragrance created will be well received by the general audience. Although the aroma of our candle lasts a long time, it is not overbearing to the point of creating headaches or nose pains. Everyone should try to buy and light a candle from Mekar Lalunna. This is since scented candles, which can lower cortisol levels, can create calm and aid to alleviate stress symptoms.

This candle consists of 4 floral scents, namely, Jasmine, Lavender, Cambodian and Rose which have their own uniqueness and advantages. The difference between our candles and others is in terms of the shape of the candle where our candles are printed using a mold and do not have a container so we can reduce the use of glass material in the process of selling candles. In addition, we use fresh flowers that we dried to be an additional fragrance to our candles which we feel is something unique and hard to find in the market. We also do the candle making process in the store where one day we only produce 98 aromatherapy candles a day. We are sure that our candles will be a visitor attraction because of the uniqueness that our store provides as well as the quality of goods that we take care of every day. Our candle sales are also limited to buyers in a day so it will definitely be the focus of the customers.
